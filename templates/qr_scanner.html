What is a QR Code Scanner?

            

                A QR Code Scanner is a tool that reads the information stored inside a QR code. When you scan a QR code using a camera or by uploading an image, the scanner decodes the black-and-white pattern and reveals the hidden content — whether it's a website link, contact details, WiFi credentials, or any other data.
            

            

                QR Dada's free online QR code scanner works directly in your browser. You don't need to install any app or create an account. Simply open this page, point your camera at a QR code, and the result appears instantly. If you have a screenshot or saved image of a QR code, you can upload it and our scanner will decode it for you.
            

            

                Our scanner uses advanced detection technology that can read standard black-and-white QR codes as well as custom-designed QR codes with colors, gradients, logos, and rounded patterns. Whether the QR code was created with QR Dada or any other platform, our scanner will decode it accurately.
