What is an Image QR Code?

An Image QR Code links directly to an uploaded image. When someone scans it, the image is displayed on their device instantly. No apps needed, no extra steps. Just scan and view.

This is ideal for sharing product photos, restaurant menus, event posters, artwork, or any visual content without printing or emailing large files.

Pro Tip: Use high-quality images for the best viewing experience. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats with a maximum file size of 5MB.

Can I update the image after creating the QR code?

Yes! If you create a dynamic QR code, you can replace the image anytime without changing the QR code itself.

Do viewers need an app to see the image?

No. The image opens directly in the viewer's web browser. No special app is needed.
