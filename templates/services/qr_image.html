What is an Image QR Code?

An Image QR Code is a QR code that opens a specific image when someone scans it. Instead of sending image files or sharing links manually, people can simply scan and view the image instantly.

This is one of the easiest ways to share visual content quickly, especially when you want to present product photos, posters, artwork, or infographics directly through a scan.

Image QR codes are widely used because they allow businesses and creators to display visual information without printing large graphics. You can place them on posters, product packaging, event materials, brochures, or marketing campaigns, and users can view the image immediately on their phone.

Pro Tip: Use dynamic QR codes so you can update the image anytime and track how many people scanned it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change the image after creating the QR code?

Yes. If you use a dynamic QR code, you can replace the image anytime without creating a new QR code. Static QR codes cannot be edited once generated.

Do image QR codes expire?

Static QR codes do not expire. Dynamic QR codes stay active as long as your QR Dada subscription remains active.

Can I track how many people scanned my image QR code?

Yes. Dynamic QR codes allow you to track scan counts, locations, devices, and timestamps.

What's the best size for printing image QR codes?

A minimum size of 2 × 2 cm works for close scanning. For posters, banners, or large displays, increase the size based on viewing distance to ensure easy scanning.
