What is a Call QR Code?

A Call QR Code stores your phone number inside a QR. When someone scans a QR Code for Call, their phone opens the dialer with your number ready. They just tap call. No typing, no mistakes. This helps you get more calls from posters, cards, packaging, and ads.

Call QR codes make it easier for people to reach you, and they reduce wrong number issues. If your business depends on phone support or sales, this helps you stay reachable without extra effort.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the call be initiated automatically?

No. For safety, scanning opens the dialer with your number ready. The user taps the call button to place the call.

Can I track how many people scanned to call?

You will know from incoming calls. If you want scan counts and locations, use our dynamic QR codes with tracking.

Do call QR codes work on tablets without phone capability?

Yes. On tablets, it may open apps like FaceTime, Skype, or other calling options if available.

Should I include country codes?

Yes. Always add your country code, like +91 or +1, so international users can call without issues.
