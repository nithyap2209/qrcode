What is a Text QR Code?

A Text QR Code stores plain text inside a QR. When someone scans a qr code for text, the text shows up instantly on their phone, even without internet. We built this as a clean text qr code generator so you can share written info in the quickest way.

Text QR codes are one of the most useful QR types because they let you share short or long text in a compact code. If you want a reliable qr code generator for text, this is one of the easiest options you can use.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much text can I put in a QR code?

You can add a lot of text, up to around 2,900 characters. For smoother scanning, we suggest keeping it short, around 300 characters or less.

Can I edit the text after creating the QR code?

Static text QR codes cannot be edited. If you need changes, generate a new qr code for text. If you want editable content, use dynamic QR codes.

Do text QR codes expire?

No. Static text QR codes never expire and will keep working.

Can I track scans of text QR codes?

Static text QR codes do not support tracking. If you want scan data, use our dynamic QR codes.

What format should I download my text QR code in?

We support PNG for digital use, SVG for print and resizing, and PDF for professional printing. Choose what fits your need.
