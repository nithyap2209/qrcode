What is an Event QR Code?

An Event QR Code stores all your event details in a calendar format. When someone scans a QR Code for an Event, their phone opens a ready calendar entry they can save right away. We built this as an easy event QR code generator so you can share events without making people type anything.

Event QR codes reduce typing errors and help people keep the right details saved. Many teams use our QR code generator for events, for conferences, workshops, classes, and public events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which calendar apps support event QR codes?

Event QR codes work with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, and any app that supports iCal or vCalendar.

Can I include meeting links in event QR codes?

Yes. You can add Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams links in the description or location.

Can I update event details after creating the QR code?

Static event QR codes cannot be updated. If you want edits later, use our dynamic QR codes.

Do event QR codes work for multi day events?

Yes. You can set start and end dates and times for multi day events.
