Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QR Dada.

What's the difference between static and dynamic QR codes?

Static QR codes stay fixed after you create them. Dynamic QR codes let you edit the content later, and they also give you tracking and scan data.

Can I change my QR code after creating it?

Yes. With dynamic QR codes, you can update the link or content anytime without reprinting. This helps when you create qr code once and want to reuse it for new campaigns.

What stats can I see for my QR codes?

You can see scans over time, locations, devices used, and basic engagement details so you know what is working.
